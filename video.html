Videography

I learned how to shoot and edit a-roll and b-roll, interview on camera with different shot sequences and capture moments to display their atmosphere. I started out with basic editing in iMovie and am continuing on to Final Cut Pro X for my next project, which I will be shooting for my video storytelling class as I study abroad in Paris and London this May.
